Smoked Salmon and Cream Cheese Crackers

Why it's so good!

Low in calories and high in protein, these crackers can serve as a small snack or a quick lunch. The smokiness of the salmon goes beautifully with the rich cream cheese and the cool, bright cucumber. A classic combination that needs no refinement.

Ingredients

  • Dry crackers
  • Smoked salmon
  • Cream cheese
  • Cucumber

Steps

  1. Take the smoked salmon out of the fridge and leave to rest for ten minutes.
  2. Spread the cream cheese on the crackers.
  3. Slice cucumber thinly and place on or two slices - depending on the size of the crackers you're using - on each cracker.
  4. Top with smoked salmon.
  5. Garnish with freshly ground black pepper.
